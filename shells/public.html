Let the crowd shape the show.

Sway gives performers, DJs, bartenders, and live venues a QR-powered way to collect requests, boosts, shoutouts, and tips.

Sway is currently in pre-production. We are opening controlled early access while backend persistence, identity, moderation, and payment safety gates are finalized.

What it does

QR-based song requests, boosts, shoutouts, tips, and live queue control in high-energy venue environments.

Who it is for

  • DJs: song requests and crowd boosts
  • Performers: paid crowd interactions
  • Bartenders: premium action menus
  • Venues: controlled audience engagement

Safety and trust

Built with moderation, blocking, durable request state, and role-based authorization before live payments.
