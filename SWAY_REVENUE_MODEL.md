# Sway Revenue Model

## Primary Revenue Streams

- Platform fee per paid request, boost, or tip.
- Performer-paid subscription for advanced queue tools, analytics, and reusable gig kits.
- Featured placement for venue discovery once real marketplace data exists.
- Venue or event operator plans for multi-performer reporting.

## Fee Rules

The app must only show fee language that matches the implemented payment processor flow. If the system captures immediately, copy must say so. If it authorizes first and captures later, the backend ledger and processor state must prove that behavior.

## Launch Constraint

Featured placement and venue discovery should remain limited until performer listings are backed by real accounts and active gigs. Public revenue claims should be avoided until payment and payout reporting is production-grade.
